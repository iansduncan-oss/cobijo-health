## Source note
The underlying facts come from hospital financial-assistance policies published by the California
Department of Health Care Access and Information (HCAI) under the Hospital Fair Pricing Act — public
records. Those source facts are not owned by anyone; **this license covers Cobijo Health's compiled,
structured, and extracted dataset** (the selection, extraction, normalization, and arrangement).
When practical, also credit HCAI as the original policy source and link to the HCAI Hospital Fair
Pricing Policy Lookup.
