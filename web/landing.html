Why this hasn't existed before

Every hospital's charity-care rules are buried in a separate, dense legal PDF. No one had ever put them in one place. Cobijo uses AI to read California hospitals' financial-assistance policies and extract each one's exact rules into one clean, open dataset — so the tool can tell you what your hospital actually offers. Open-source and open-data, built in the public interest.
